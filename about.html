Biography

Prize-winning journalist Ann Brenoff became known as a pioneer of the family caregiver rage movement as she shared her first-hand experiences as her late-husband's caregiver. She spent most of her career at the Los Angeles Times before joining HuffPost as a senior writer/editor and staff columnist. She now writes for AARP and other national publications.
